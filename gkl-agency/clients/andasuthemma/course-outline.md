## Module Breakdown

### Module 1 — Förstå din relation till röran (Understand Your Clutter)
- Why ADHD brains struggle with organization (validation, not shame)
- The emotional weight of clutter
- Identifying YOUR organizing style
- **Worksheet:** My Home Audit — what spaces stress me most?

### Module 2 — Mindset & Motivation (The Foundation)
- Why traditional organizing advice doesn't work for ADHD
- The "good enough" method vs perfectionism
- Building momentum: small wins → big change
- **Worksheet:** My Why + My One Room

### Module 3 — Vanesas Metod: Rum för Rum (Room by Room)
- The 4-step system: Sort → Decide → Place → Maintain
- Starting with the easiest room (not the worst)
- Kitchen, bedroom, wardrobe, office — specific guides
- **Worksheet:** Room-by-Room Action Plan

### Module 4 — Hållbara System (Sustainable Systems)
- Why things go back to chaos — and how to prevent it
- The "home for everything" principle
- Labels, zones, and visual cues for ADHD brains
- 10-minute daily reset routine
- **Worksheet:** My Maintenance Plan

### Module 5 — Digital Röran (Digital Clutter)
- Phone, email, desktop, photos — where to start
- ADHD-friendly digital organization tools
- Unsubscribe, declutter, and protect your attention
- **Worksheet:** Digital Detox Checklist

### Module 6 — Hålla Farten Uppe (Staying on Track)
- What to do when you fall off the wagon
- Building accountability systems
- Celebrating progress (not perfection)
- When to call in a professional organizer
- **Bonus:** Access to Vanesa's community group
