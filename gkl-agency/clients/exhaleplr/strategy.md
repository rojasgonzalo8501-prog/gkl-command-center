## What ExhalePLR Is

A premium PLR (Private Label Rights) store. Customers buy done-for-you digital content —
courses, social media kits, email sequences, ebooks — and rebrand/resell it as their own,
or use it to build their business faster.

**Why this is a great business:**
- Products created once, sold forever (100% passive after launch)
- No inventory, no shipping, no customer service issues
- Growing market: millions of coaches, consultants, and online entrepreneurs need content
- High margins (near 100% on digital products)
- Stacks perfectly with GKL Agency services — we can cross-sell

---

## The Market

**Target customer:** English-speaking solopreneurs, coaches, consultants, and new
entrepreneurs who need content but don't have time or skill to create it from scratch.

**Primary niches to serve:**
1. Business & Life Coaching (biggest PLR market)
2. Wellness & Mindfulness
3. Personal Finance & Productivity
4. Fitness & Health
5. Social Media Marketing (meta — sell content about selling content)

**The problem we solve:**
> "I know what I want to sell, but creating all the content takes forever.
> I need something I can launch NOW."

## Marketing Channels (Priority Order)

### 1. Pinterest 🏆 BIGGEST OPPORTUNITY
- PLR buyers browse Pinterest obsessively
- Pins have a 6-month+ lifespan (vs 24 hours on Instagram)
- Create 5–10 pins per product
- Boards: "PLR Content Kits", "Done-for-You Business", "Passive Income Digital Products"
- Link directly to product pages
- **Goal:** 50k monthly views in 90 days → 200+ visitors/day

### 2. Instagram / TikTok
- Content pillars:
  - "What's inside this PLR kit" (product reveals)
  - "How I made [X] passive income selling digital products"
  - "How to rebrand PLR in 10 minutes"
  - "Launch your coaching business in a weekend"
- Faceless brand works fine — show screens, mockups, results
- 3–4 Reels/week minimum

### 3. Email List (build from day 1)
- **Free lead magnet:** "10 Done-for-You Instagram Captions for Coaches" (free mini-kit)
- Pop-up on site: email to download
- Weekly newsletter: "The PLR Drop" — new product alerts + tips
- **Goal:** 500 subscribers in 60 days → $1,500+ per email blast

### 4. SEO / Blog ⚠️ FIX FIRST
- Site is JS-rendered (Lovable) — Google cannot index it
- Need to fix this before investing in content
- Once fixed: target keywords like:
  - "PLR course content for coaches" (low competition)
  - "done for you social media content"
  - "PLR business coaching content"
  - "private label rights wellness"

### 5. Facebook Groups
- Post in PLR and digital product groups (huge communities)
- Groups: "PLR and Digital Products", "Online Business Owners", "Digital Product Sellers"
- Strategy: be helpful, post value, soft-promote products
- Do NOT spam — build reputation first

### 6. Etsy (underrated channel)
- Etsy has millions of people searching for digital products and templates
- List Social Media Kits and Course Kits as Etsy products
- Drives external traffic with zero ad spend
- **One-time setup — passive discovery forever**
