Technical Fixes

Fix These Before
Spending on Marketing

No point driving traffic to a site that can't convert or can't be found. These fixes come first.

🚨 Critical
JavaScript Rendering (Zero SEO)

Site is built on Lovable (React). Google cannot read the content. Add static meta tags per product OR migrate to a static/hybrid setup. Without this, all blog and SEO investment is wasted.

🚨 Critical
No Email Capture on Site

Every visitor who leaves without buying is gone forever unless we have their email. Add a popup: "Get 10 Free Done-for-You Captions → Download Now." This list becomes our most valuable asset.

⚠ High Priority
Generic Hero Headline

"Launch Your Business Today" says nothing specific. Change to: "Done-for-You Content Kits for Coaches & Entrepreneurs. Buy, Rebrand, Launch — Today." Specific = more conversions.

⚠ High Priority
Pricing Not Visible

Visitors shouldn't have to hunt for prices. Show prices directly on product cards in the shop. Add a "Best Sellers" section to the homepage with product cards, names, and prices visible immediately.

Medium
No Social Proof

Add a testimonials section. Reach out to early buyers for a quote. Even 2–3 real testimonials with names and results dramatically increase conversion rates for new visitors.

Medium
No Social Links

Pinterest, Instagram, and TikTok links should be in the footer and header. Visitors who don't buy today can follow and become buyers later — don't lose them completely.
