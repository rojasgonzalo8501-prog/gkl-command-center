# CLAUDE.md — ExhalePLR.com

## What Is This?
**ExhalePLR** is a GKL Agency **internal project** — a premium PLR (Private Label Rights) digital product store.
Customers buy done-for-you content (courses, social media kits, email sequences, ebooks) and rebrand/resell it.

- **Website:** exhaleplr.com (Lovable-built, JS-rendered — SEO broken, needs fix)
- **Owner:** GKL Agency (Gonzalo)
- **Status:** Build phase — content and products being created

## Business Model
Products are created once, sold forever. Near-100% margins. No inventory or shipping.
This is GKL Agency's passive income stream AND a funnel to sell agency services.

**Cross-sell hook:** Every PLR buyer is a potential GKL client.
Add to every order confirmation: *"Want us to build this for you? Reply for 15% off GKL Agency services."*

## Target Customer
English-speaking solopreneurs, coaches, consultants, new entrepreneurs who need content to launch fast.

**Primary niches:**
1. Business & Life Coaching (biggest PLR market)
2. Wellness & Mindfulness
3. Personal Finance & Productivity
4. Fitness & Health
5. Social Media Marketing
