# Client Brief — GKL Agency Website

## Agency Info
- **Name**: GKL Agency
- **Owner**: Gonzalo
- **Services**: Social Media Content, Website Creation, Online Courses
- **Industries served**: Any industry (generalist digital agency)

## Website Goal
Attract businesses that need social media content, websites, or online courses. Convert visitors into leads via a contact form or CTA.

## Target Clients
- Small to medium businesses wanting to grow online
- Entrepreneurs launching courses or personal brands
- Local businesses needing a professional web presence
- Startups needing marketing content and websites

## Style & Design
- Professional, modern, premium feel
- Dark background (deep navy #0A0E1A) with gold/white accents
- Clean typography, bold headlines, smooth animations
- Mobile-first, fully responsive

## Pages
1. **Home** — Hero, Services overview, Why GKL, Social proof section, CTA
2. **Services** — Deep dive into Social Media, Websites, Courses
3. **About** — Gonzalo's story, agency mission
4. **Contact** — Contact form, email, social links

## Brand Colors
- Primary: #0A0E1A (deep navy)
- Accent: #C9A84C (gold)
- Text: #FFFFFF / #E0E0E0
- Cards: #141824
- CTA buttons: Gold with hover effect
