## Background
Artemis was a competitive figure skater from age 3–15. After moving to Sweden and becoming a mother of two, she discovered yoga. She named the brand "Yalo" — Greek for "seashore" — inspired by witnessing Mediterranean plastic pollution threatening her children's future. She chose cork as her hero material: eco-friendly, antibacterial, biodegradable.
