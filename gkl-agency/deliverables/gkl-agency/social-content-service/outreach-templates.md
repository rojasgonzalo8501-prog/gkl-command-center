# Outreach templates — content-calendar service

> Lead with the pain, keep it short, one clear ask. English + Swedish (many GKL prospects are
> Swedish — match the prospect's language). Fill `[brackets]` before sending.

> Totally normal — running the place is the actual job. I run done-for-you content calendars for
> businesses like yours: I write, design and post everything, you never touch it. Want me to send
> a sample month for [business] so you can see the quality? No cost to look.

## Personal brand / coach (EN, short)
> Your face is the business — but repurposing your own content across channels is a full-time job
> you don't have time for. Send me your raw content once a month (a talk, a livestream, a few
> voice notes) and I'll turn it into a month of posts, carousels and reels across every channel.
> Fixed monthly price, you approve, I post. Want a sample?

---

## The three-tier close (use after they're interested)
> Three ways to run it:
> **Starter (2 995 kr/mån)** — one channel, ~12 posts, captions written for you. Great to start.
> **Growth (5 995 kr/mån)** — 2–3 channels, ~20 posts incl. carousels + reels, auto-published.
> **Done-for-you (9 995 kr/mån)** — everything above + strategy, approvals, reporting. You never touch it.
>
> Most start on Starter to see it work, then move to Growth. Which fits where you are right now?

## Notes
- Always offer a **sample month** (a real batch, like the Vivanord one) — showing beats telling.
- Never lead with "AI". Lead with their pain; the delivery method is yours to keep.
- Prices are defaults for the Swedish SMB market — adjust per prospect; compliance-heavy niches +20–40%.
